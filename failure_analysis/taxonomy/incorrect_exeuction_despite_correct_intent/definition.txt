The agent correctly understands the task objective and the data semantics, but implements the solution incorrectly due to errors in logic, syntax, or algorithmic realization.

Necessary & sufficient conditions of this FM:
- The agent’s plan is conceptually correct
- The relevant data semantics are known
- The produced code/query/logic is faulty

This FM excludes:
- Instruction or interface violations
- Solving the wrong problem (objective deviation)
- Unawareness of schema or data semantics

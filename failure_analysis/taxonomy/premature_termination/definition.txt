The agent halts before collecting sufficient information to correctly answer the task, either
- Voluntarily: The agent halts early without encountering an explicit, unresolved error.
- Reactively: The agent abandons the task after encountering errors, without
recovering or attempting alternative strategies.

Necessary & sufficient conditions of this FM:
- The agent terminates with an answer or no answer
- The termination occurs before sufficient data is gathered
- The insufficiency is not required by the task

This FM excludes:
- Incorrect reasoning using complete information
- Loss of information after complete information was collected
- Wrong objective or schema misunderstanding
